ABOUT SEASON

4 SEASON

In many parts of the world, the year is made up of four different seasons. These seasons are spring, summer, fall, and winter. Each has its own pattern of weather and varying hours of sunlight during the days. The northern and southern parts of Earth have different seasons at the same time. For example, when it is winter in Europe in the northern part, it is summer in Australia in the southern part. This difference is due to which part of Earth is nearest the sun at a certain time of the year. However, places around the middle of Earth have one hot season all year or only two seasons, dry and rainy.
